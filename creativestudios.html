Creative Studios – A Fusion of Music and Visual Art

At Wrightson Ventures, we support businesses that push creative boundaries. Our Creative Studios ventures include music and visual art. With a record label featuring various artists and a dedicated art print platform.

The Record Label

Our record label is a base for a variety of genres. We believe in supporting music that speaks to a global audience. We provide artists with the resources and management to develop their productions and reach new listeners.

Art - Prints That Inspire

Beyond music, our Creative Studios also curates a unique collection of art prints, offering high-quality artwork. Our sales platform serves as a digital gallery where customers can purchase prints that resonate with their personal style, whether inspired by music, culture, or abstract creativity.

From AI-generated designs to hand-drawn illustrations and photography, our prints represent a diverse spectrum of artistic expression. Each piece is produced with premium materials, ensuring that every purchase is both a statement and an investment in creative talent.

A Vision for the Future

Creative Studios is more than just a label or an art platform—it’s a movement that fosters creativity, collaboration, and innovation. As we continue to grow, we aim to expand our network of artists, develop new ways to showcase music and visual art, and create meaningful experiences for audiences around the world.

Through Wrightson Ventures, our Creative Studios has the backing to become a hub for emerging talent, breaking down traditional industry barriers and redefining what it means to be an independent creative brand.

Join the Creative Revolution

Whether you’re an artist looking for a platform, a musician searching for the right label, or an art lover wanting to explore new prints, Creative Studios is the place for you.
