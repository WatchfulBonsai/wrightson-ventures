Dolphin Mobile – Sustainable Connectivity for a Better Future

At Wrightson Ventures, we invest in businesses that drive innovation and sustainability. Dolphin Mobile is our forward-thinking telecommunications venture, committed to providing eco-friendly and affordable SIM card solutions in Germany. With a focus on sustainability and customer-centric services, Dolphin Mobile is redefining mobile connectivity.

Eco-Friendly SIM Solutions

In a world where environmental responsibility is more important than ever, Dolphin Mobile is leading the way with sustainable mobile services. We provide SIM cards made from recycled materials, promote paperless activation processes, and ensure that our operations align with eco-conscious business practices.

Our goal is to offer seamless connectivity while reducing electronic waste and promoting green alternatives in the telecom industry. Whether for individuals or businesses, Dolphin Mobile ensures that staying connected doesn’t come at the cost of the environment.

Affordable and Reliable Mobile Services

Alongside our sustainability efforts, Dolphin Mobile delivers cost-effective, high-quality mobile plans designed to meet diverse customer needs. Our services include:

  • Flexible Prepaid and Contract Plans: Affordable options with no hidden fees.
  • Nationwide Coverage: Reliable connectivity across Germany.
  • eSIM Technology: Hassle-free digital activation for modern devices.
  • International Roaming: Seamless mobile access when travelling abroad.
  • Business Solutions: Custom mobile packages for enterprises and professionals.

Why Choose Dolphin Mobile?

As part of Wrightson Ventures, Dolphin Mobile combines innovation with ethical business practices. We believe that mobile services should be affordable, reliable, and environmentally responsible. That’s why we’re committed to offering high-quality network coverage and exceptional customer support while actively working towards a greener future.

Whether you’re an individual looking for an affordable SIM card or a business seeking scalable mobile solutions, Dolphin Mobile is the right choice for sustainable and reliable connectivity.
