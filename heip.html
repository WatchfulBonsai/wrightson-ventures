>HEIP™ – Empowering Local Entrepreneurs and Community Projects

At Wrightson Ventures, we recognise that some of the most impactful businesses and community initiatives start at the grassroots level. That’s why we’ve developed HEIP – the Hometown Entrepreneurs and Investment Platform.

HEIP is an innovative investment and support platform designed to empower local entrepreneurs, businesses, and community leaders to turn their ideas into reality.

A Platform for Innovation and Growth

HEIP is more than just an investment initiative—it’s a collaborative space where local people can set up detailed plans for a business, event, or social project and gain financial or strategic support through a milestone-based investment system. Whether it’s a startup, an expansion of an existing business, or a social impact project, HEIP connects visionaries with the backing they need to grow and succeed.

How HEIP Works

HEIP provides a structured approach to community-driven growth by allowing participants to:

  • Pitch Business or Project Ideas: Entrepreneurs and community leaders submit their business, event, or social project proposals.
  • Set Clear Milestones: Approved participants outline key milestones that will guide the development of their initiative.
  • Gain Investment and Support: HEIP provides financial backing, mentorship, and resources based on milestone completion.
  • Ensure Long-Term Sustainability: The structured investment model encourages accountability and continued growth.

Supporting Local Entrepreneurs, Building Stronger Communities

HEIP is designed to encourage innovation and sustainability at the local level, ensuring that great ideas don’t go unrealised due to a lack of funding or support. By focusing on milestone-based investment, HEIP helps mitigate risk for investors while providing reliable, structured funding for entrepreneurs and project leaders.
